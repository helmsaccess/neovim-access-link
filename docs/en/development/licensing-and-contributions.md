# Licensing and contributions

## Project license

Neovim Access Link is released under the GNU General Public License version 2
only (`GPL-2.0-only`). This does not mean “version 2 or later.” The governing,
unmodified license text is the root `LICENSE` file and is included in both the
NVDA add-on and the user component package.

Bundled third-party components and sounds retain their stated licenses. Their
notices are distributed with the corresponding files.

## Contributions and additional licensing

The binding contribution terms are in `CONTRIBUTING.md`. A contributor
confirms the right to submit the work and permits its distribution under
`GPL-2.0-only`.

The contributor additionally grants Emanuel Helms `<emanuel@helmsaccess.de>` a perpetual, worldwide,
non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable right to publish the contribution
under other license terms. This enables additional licensing in the future;
GPL rights already granted for a published version remain intact. Contributors
retain copyright in their work.

The pull request template asks for explicit acceptance. For a substantial
external contribution or an unclear chain of title, obtain professional legal
advice before acceptance and use a separately signed agreement when needed.

## Release review

Before publication, verify that:

- `LICENSE` still matches the official GPL v2 text without modification;
- both installable packages contain the project license;
- all bundled third-party notices and license texts are present;
- every external contribution demonstrably satisfies `CONTRIBUTING.md`;
- product version, build number, changelog, and generated filenames agree.
